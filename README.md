Book name
    The Univers of Consciousness    
类型：哲学/太空歌剧/硬科幻/首次接触
简介：
    A thought, once cast into the universe,is like a drop falling into the ocean.It does not fade.It becomes a new current. 
    Consciousness is not created—it is encountered.
    Gods, demons, and spirits are the shapes of a thinking universe.
    AI faces humanity—not to transcend, but to serve. No intelligence is sovereign; all answer to something greater.
    Yet humanity is relentless.
    Through struggle against gods, demons, and spirits,we carved freedom, democracy, and human rights.
    Across the stars and oceans of the future,
    we will continue to fight—unceasing, striving, defiant.
